Think about your work, your school, your circle of friends. Do they have traits that are similar to you? Are they homogeneous? What keeps you from embracing diversity?

All people have blind spots, or implicit biases, that steer us to people who are more like ourselves and away from us who are not. We hope that this project will help you to become aware of your biases that inhibit you from experiencing difference.

Change.

Be inclusive towards those who are different than yourself. Reflect. How has diversity positively impacted the world around you?
